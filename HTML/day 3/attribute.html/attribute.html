About Royal Enfield

Established in 1901, Royal Enfield is the oldest motorcycle brand in continuous production, carrying a legacy that spans over 125 years. Born in Redditch, England, and refined in Chennai, India, we have evolved from a military supplier to a global icon of leisure motorcycling. Our philosophy, "Made Like a Gun," reflects our commitment to building simple, resilient, and dependable machines that stand the test of time. From the rugged trails of the Himalayas to the urban streets of London, our motorcycles are crafted for those who seek the journey over the destination. We don't just build engines; we build an enduring culture of exploration, freedom, and authentic character. With iconic models like the Bullet, Classic, and Himalayan, we continue to lead the way in modern-classic motorcycling worldwide. Join us as we celebrate a century of heritage, craftsmanship, and the unhurried pursuit of the open road.

Comfortable Riding Experience

Riding a Royal Enfield is defined by a relaxed, upright posture that keeps your spine neutral and minimizes fatigue on long journeys. The bikes are designed with wide handlebars and mid-set footpegs, ensuring you don’t have to hunch over or feel cramped while navigating traffic. Most models feature generously padded, wide seats that provide excellent support for both the rider and a passenger over several hours. While older versions were known for their heavy vibrations, the newer J-Series engines are remarkably smooth due to advanced counter-balancing technology. The heavy, planted feel of the chassis offers a sense of stability and confidence, especially when cruising at steady speeds. This combination of a low center of gravity and a soft suspension setup allows the bike to soak up road bumps with ease.
